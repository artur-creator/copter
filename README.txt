Turret has 3 HP, deals 10 hp damage
Tracking player's position by overlaping detection sphere. For visual detectiond can be used Line Trace and Get distance, but I used Overlap, because we have no obstacles and we don't need high precision
Shooting while player is near
Stops when player flew away or dead

Player has 100 HP and 100 ammo

Medkit heals 15 
Ammo refills 10 

On death game restarts

Controls:
WASD, Space(Go up), CTRL(Go down), Mouse to look, LMB to shot
